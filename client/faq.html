Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Vaibhly

1. What is Vaibhly?
Vaibhly is a comprehensive online learning and career platform designed to bridge the gap between education and real-world job opportunities.

The platform enables learners to:
• Learn industry-relevant skills through structured courses
• Validate their knowledge through assessments and exams
• Earn verifiable certificates with unique IDs and QR codes
• Connect with recruiters and apply for relevant job opportunities

Unlike traditional e-learning platforms, Vaibhly focuses on measurable outcomes — ensuring that learners not only acquire knowledge but can also prove their skills in a credible way.
2. Are Vaibhly certificates recognized and valid?
Yes. All certificates issued by Vaibhly are digitally verifiable.

Each certificate includes:
• A unique certificate ID
• A QR code for instant verification
• Secure backend validation

Recruiters and organizations can verify the authenticity of a certificate directly through the platform, ensuring transparency and trust.

However, recognition may vary depending on employer preferences, and Vaibhly does not guarantee universal acceptance by all organizations.
3. Do I need to complete the course to receive a certificate?
Yes. Vaibhly follows a strict quality-based certification model.

To receive a certificate, you must:
• Complete the course content
• Attempt and pass the required assessments or final exam

This ensures that certificates represent actual knowledge and skill proficiency rather than simple course participation.
4. How many attempts are allowed for exams?
The number of attempts allowed varies depending on the course or exam configuration.

In general:
• Some exams allow multiple attempts to improve performance
• Others may have strict limits to maintain integrity

Attempt limits are clearly defined before you start the exam.
5. Can I access courses after purchasing?
Yes. Once you purchase a course, it will be available in your dashboard.

You can:
• Access lessons anytime
• Revisit content as needed
• Continue learning at your own pace

Access duration may vary depending on the course terms.
6. How does Vaibhly help in getting jobs?
Vaibhly connects learners with recruiters through a structured ecosystem.

Learners who complete courses and earn certificates can:
• Apply to jobs posted by recruiters
• Showcase verified skills
• Improve their credibility in hiring decisions

While Vaibhly facilitates job connections, it does not guarantee employment.
7. Can I become an instructor on Vaibhly?
Yes. Vaibhly allows professionals and experts to become instructors. You can create courses, design assessments, and earn revenue while building your personal brand.
8. How do recruiters use Vaibhly?
Recruiters can post job opportunities and access a pool of certified candidates. Since learners are evaluated through exams, recruiters can trust the skills of candidates more effectively.
9. Is my data safe?
Yes. Vaibhly follows standard security practices to protect your data. We do not sell personal information and ensure that your account details remain secure.
10. Can I get a refund?
Refunds are subject to course-specific policies. Generally, refunds may be considered in cases such as technical issues or accidental duplicate payments.
11. How can I contact support?
You can reach out through the contact page or email support. Our team aims to respond within 24 hours to resolve your queries.
